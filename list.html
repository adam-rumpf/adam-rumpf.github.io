Over the years I've generated quite the smattering of random math and math-adjacent projects released for free online. I've been very fortunate to have had the opportunity to work on whatever open-source and open-access projects interest me at the moment, and in spite of having chosen a career path that actively discourages having a wide range of interests, I fully intend to continue doing so as long as I'm still breathing. I decided to collect a list of all of these projects in one place because writing down everything you've ever done in your life is a perfectly normal and cool thing to do.
